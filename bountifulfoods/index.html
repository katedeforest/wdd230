Carlsbad

Carlsbad, located along the picturesque coastline of northern San Diego County, California, is a vibrant city with a population of around 115,000 people. Renowned for its stunning beaches, including Carlsbad State Beach and South Carlsbad State Beach, the city is a popular tourist destination. Carlsbad boasts a diverse economy, with a focus on technology, tourism, healthcare, and manufacturing, including the presence of the Carlsbad Research Center. Families are drawn to the area for its excellent school system, served by the Carlsbad Unified School District, and there are also higher education opportunities at MiraCosta College. Beyond its beautiful natural scenery, Carlsbad offers attractions such as LEGOLAND California Resort, the Flower Fields at Carlsbad Ranch, and the historic Leo Carrillo Ranch. The city enjoys a Mediterranean climate, with mild winters and warm summers, making it an appealing year-round destination for residents and visitors alike.
