Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Netflix

https://www.netflix.com/

Visual hierarchy is the principle of arranging elements to show their order of importance and help users understand information easily.

Netflix does this well by having their main purpose in large text in the center of the screen. Then the viewer's eye may flow to the bright red elements on the page which point out significant details, and then to the smaller white text which provide further details.

Hick's Law

Mo' Bettahs

https://mobettahs.com/

Hicks Law is used to simplify choices and remove barriers in decision-making. Deciding which menu item to get can be difficult.

Mo' Bettahs simplifies this choice on their website by having only one menu item on their main page and telling the viewer to try that item.

White Space
and Clean Design

Duolingo

https://www.duolingo.com/

Clean design typically means uncluttered and simple. It can mean removing distractions and focusing on the content, the key message, and the most important action.

Duolingo does this by removing all unimportant elements from their main page. They simply have some engaging illustrations, a little text to tell you the purpose of their tool, and provide a button to sign in or get started. There is plenty of white space around these elements to help draw attention to them.
