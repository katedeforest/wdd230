The "Real" Up House

Step into the enchanting world of the real-life 'Up' house, a charming homage to the beloved Pixar film. This whimsical abode, located in a quiet neighborhood, defies gravity with a colorful array of balloons, just like in the movie. The house's vibrant facade, adorned with hundreds of balloons, evokes a sense of adventure and nostalgia, inviting visitors to relive the heartwarming tale of Carl and Ellie. A true work of art and a testament to the enduring magic of animation, the real 'Up' house is a sight to behold and a reminder that dreams can take flight, no matter your age.

Rio Tinto Kennecott Visitor Experience

Discover the fascinating world of mining history and the unparalleled Rio Tinto Kennecott Visitor Experience. Nestled amidst the awe-inspiring landscapes of Utah, this immersive attraction offers a unique opportunity to delve into the rich heritage of one of the world's largest man-made excavation sites. From panoramic viewpoints that reveal the immense scale of the mine to interactive exhibits that bring the story of copper mining to life, the Visitor Experience is a captivating journey through time and industry. Get ready to be awed by the sheer magnitude of this iconic site and gain a newfound appreciation for the legacy of human achievement against the backdrop of nature's grandeur.

The Ice Ribbon at J. Lynn Crane Plaza

Embrace the winter wonderland as you behold the glistening beauty of The Ice Ribbon at J. Lynn Crane Plaza. This enchanting ice skating rink, set against the backdrop of an urban landscape, is a true marvel of seasonal delight. Twinkling lights and the crisp, frosty air create a magical atmosphere for all ages to enjoy. Whether you're a seasoned skater or taking your first glide, The Ice Ribbon offers a perfect blend of festive joy and chilly elegance. Lace up your skates and let the ice beneath your feet carry you into the heart of a winter fairytale.

Blackridge Reservoir

Nature's tranquil masterpiece unfolds at Blackridge Reservoir, where serenity meets scenic beauty. This hidden gem in Utah beckons with its glassy waters, framed by the lush greenery of the surrounding landscape. As the sun's warm embrace dances upon the surface, it's a perfect spot for relaxation, recreation, and quiet contemplation. Whether you're picnicking by the shore or paddleboarding on the calm waters, Blackridge Reservoir is a sanctuary for those seeking a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Herriman Library

Step inside the Herriman Library, a modern haven of knowledge and community in the heart of this charming city. With its welcoming architecture and inviting atmosphere, this library offers a space for exploration, learning, and connection. The shelves are filled with a world of stories, and the cozy reading nooks provide the perfect escape. Whether you're a bookworm, a curious learner, or simply seeking a quiet place to unwind, Herriman Library is a delightful destination where imagination knows no bounds, and the love of reading is celebrated.

J.L. Sorenson Recreation Center

Dive into the vibrant world of the J.L. Sorenson Recreation Center, where fitness and fun converge under one modern roof. This state-of-the-art facility is a hub of activity and wellness, catering to all ages and interests. From the gleaming swimming pools that invite you to make a splash to the well-equipped fitness areas, it's a place where health and recreation come together seamlessly. The lively atmosphere and dynamic programs make it a community treasure, where individuals and families can pursue their fitness goals and create lasting memories. Whether you're hitting the gym, shooting hoops, or enjoying quality family time, the J.L. Sorenson Recreation Center is the ultimate destination for a vibrant and active lifestyle.
